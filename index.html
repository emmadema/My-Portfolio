My Closet

The app that helps you pick out an outfit.

Have you ever stood in front of your closet hopelessly starring into the endless void of clothes. Are you continuously late to events, because you have to try on every outfit in your closet, just to settle on the first one you picked out? If you answered yet to either of these questions then My Closet is the app for you! My Closet helps you pick an outfit based on clothing you already have in your closet. It scans the Shop Style API to find the perfect outfit based on the weather in your area. Thanks to My Closet you will never miss and event again.

  • Date: October 2017
  • HTML, CSS, JS, Jquery, NodeJS, Mongo, Express

swipe.Bite

A Tinder style app which helps you find food near you.

Do you spend a lot of time looking for food on the go? Can you not eat at a restaurant unless you have checked all possible reviews to make sure everyone else has liked it? swipe.Bite pulls in all the restaurants near you and allows you to swipe through them. If you like a restaurant you can get directions and save it to your favorites list.

  • Date: November 2017
  • Angular 2+, ngBootstrap, NodeJS, npm, PSQL

Rugrats Matching Game

A matching game with all of your favorite Rugrats characters.

Are you a 90's kid through and through? Did you run off the bus each afternoon just to make it home in time to see Rugrats on Nickelodeon? Me too! Thats why I made the Rugrats matching game. This game is a one or two player matching game featuring Angelica, tommy and the rest of the game.

  • Date: September 2017
  • Languages: HTML, CSS, JS, Jquery
